Outbreaks

Greetings, Cybersleuths! Below is a list Digimon of the nefarious digimon that have caused havoc in our world at one point in time, now quarantined to our virus chest. Normally, we can contain them without issue, but sometimes they escape. When they do, we go after them again immediately. We also post the annoucement on the outbreaks page for those that wish to assist us with that.
For those looking to fill out their Digidex, we, unfortunately, we can't let you into the chest for safety reasons; we can only go as far as posting their images (as they were last seen as) and the data we have gathered on them.

That said, scanning these QR codes is a safe way to add them to your Digidex

Impmon:

The Imp Digimon

 Impmon's namesake is from the mythological creature: the Imp. Imp are said to be mischevous, fond of playing pranks on people. True their namesake, Impmon enjoys pullings pranks on humans; true to their namesake, they do it for attention. Impmon are lonely digimon, so they pull pranks for recognition.

*While causing mischief doesn't necessarily make it dangerous, some of its pranks are harmful, such as switching babies at birth and leading people to become lost in unfamiliar places, hence his containment here in the chest.
Myotismon Myotis is a known menace in the Digital world, where he, like a Vampire, feeds off the fresh or deleted data of other Digimon. However, when he found his way to the human world, Myotismon developed a taste for human blood during his stay in Odaiba in Japan, causing his victims to appear to have been afflicted with sudden anemia. It took over a dozen human and Digimon teams to capture him
Parrotmon Parrotmon is a Bird Digimon whose name and design are derived from the Parrot. It appeared from a Digital World in another dimension, straying off from the world through an empty rip of space-time. its sharp claws and strong electrical charge are assumed to be its weaponry.

We do not yet know much about it.
