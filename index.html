What are Digimon?

There exists world separate from ours, a virtual world. In this world, where data is given shape. There, data takes on the form of sentient beings known as Digital Monsters, or Digimon. For the most part, they keep to themselves, but there are some who would seek to do our world harm. They plot to take over our world through technology; smartphones, TVs, cars, planes, you name it.

That is where we come in.

Who We Are

The Digimon Cybersleuth Detective Agency is made up of humans and Digimon working together to protect our homes. The more operatives we have in the field, the merrier, so don't hesitate to apply and expand our network!
